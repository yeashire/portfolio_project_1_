About Anna

I am a qualified psychotherapist practising in North Dublin. I hold an Masters Degree in Counselling & Psychotherapy (Integrative Modalities) from The Irish College of Humanities & Applied Science (ICHAS). I am also an accredited member of APCP and NAPCP.


I work with a range of issues including depression, anxiety, anger, grief, relationships, self-esteem, self-harm, suicidal ideation and have a particular interest in working with individuals who have experienced any form of trauma.


I am specialazing in IFS (Internal Family Systems). I work from an integrative perspective I use several different therapeutic modalities, including Humansitic, Choice Theory, Existential, psychodynamic and CBT.


I am guided by my appreciation for the client and his willingness to make changes in his life. Therapy takes place through dialogue, in a safe atmosphere based on conditions agreed individually with the client. Each meeting creates situations where you can experience support for yourself.
